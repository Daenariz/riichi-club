About Our Club

We are an unofficial Riichi Mahjong community at the Technical University. Whether you are aiming for a Yakuman or just want to learn the basics, you are welcome!

Why Kokushi Musou?

Named after the legendary "Thirteen Orphans" hand, we believe in the beauty of assembling something extraordinary from scattered tiles.
